On the usage of Co-occurences.html:

1) In the Chord Diagram the co-occurrences of the most frequent Named Entities for each time period are depicted.

2) Select the appropriate time period from the drop down menu.

3) Named Entities of type LOCATION are colored blue, PERSON Red and ORGANIZATION green.

4) The positioning of the words in the circumference of the diagram is analogous to their frequency. 
   Starting at 12:00 is the most frequent Named Entity for the selected time period, followed by the second most frequent, etc..
   
5) The width of the each link is proportional to the percentage of the co-occurences alloted to the pair source-target, with regards to
   the overall co-occurences of the source entity.
